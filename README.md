# Saint Botolph's Missal
The *Saint Botolph's Missal* provides everything necessary for the celebration of the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass throughout the Year, according to the English Orthodox liturgical tradition within the Antiochian Western Rite Vicariate.

# Sources
The *Saint Botolph's Missal* contains the parts for the Mass from the 2025 Proposed Book of Common Prayer. In addition to this, it supplies everything else from the 1933 English Missal. Any exceptions to this are listed in the Copyright section of the book. The 2025 Proposed Book of Common Prayer itself is a conservative revision of the 1928 Book of Common Prayer with supplementation from the Monastic Breviary and the 1933 English Missal, based on the needs for Orthodox use, consulting the Russian *Observations* and the *Orthodox Edict*.

The worship of the Church is properly the Church's. Because of this, we have decided to not lock our publications behind strict copyright restrictions. The LaTeX code is under the GNU GPL 3.0. The PDF and print publications are under the Creative Commons BY-SA 4.0 license. This means that you are free to use these as you wish. We are joyful to share the beauty of the American Orthodox tradition to all interested in Christ's Religion.
